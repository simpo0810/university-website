Our Global Campus

KDU Global Campus offers various undergraduate degree programs in both English and Korean, language and vocational training courses for international students. All undergraduate curriculums are designed to incorporate five major specialization tracks simultaneously as well as liberal arts courses to enhance the language and soft skills of the students. The ultimate mission of the Campus is to guarantee the professional and personal success of the graduates by equipping them with the competencies sought after in the industries and communities of the 21st century

Commitment to liberal arts & sciences is at the core of Global campus's mission: before students can help change the world, they need to understand it. The liberal arts & sciences offer a broad intellectual foundation for the tools to think critically, reason analytically and write clearly.
